Hi, I'm Scarlett, nice to meet you!

Born and raised in New York, I have always thrived in a fast paced lifestyle, always encouraging different challenges to overcome. After multiple years working in the healthcare industry, I realized that I would not have much of a work-life balance long term and ultimately be unhappy. Being that I always loved coding, from different IT courses in highschool and even customizing my MySpace page, I decided to pursue a career in IT without any regret.
With both a keen eye for detail and simplistic taste, I am excited to help contribute to any team or working individually with any client from every walk of life. I like to push the boundaries in anything I do, always remaining ambitious and flexible to obstacles thrown my way. My intention is to capture any collaborator’s personality and develop it into an interactive interface.

I love spending time outdoors,anything music related, traveling, trying new foods, and immersing myself in different cultures. I hope to be able to open enough doors where I can collaborate on a global scale.
